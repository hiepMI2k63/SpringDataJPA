The website allows to manage car park employees, operation
administration, parking registration, booking office, check in/ check out of
cars, car trip and parking lot.

Human Resource
Management staff
The staff has right to search, add, delete, edit employee
information, statistic report
Car park The staff has right to:
- Manage parking registration, including searching, adding,
deleting, editing, and reporting statistics.
- Manage booking office, including searching, adding, deleting,
editing ticket information and reporting statistics.
- Manage car park, including adding, deleting, editing car park
information.
